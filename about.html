About Cranode

Cranode's goal is to give everyone the tools to create anything imaginable.
We allow the community to fully develop programs for users to create
projects. Individuals who are experts or beginners in their domain can easily
start and publish their work through different versions of programs. Finally
we give users the opportunity to send their work to global digital outlets such
as app stores, publishers, media companies, and ecommerce stores.
